The place for hollidays

The "Rhune"

The "Rhune" is the higher mountain of the Pays-Basque and is located near Hendaye. Is climbing tooks near 3 hours and offer (when the weather is not cloudy) a beautiful view on Hendaye.

The beach

Hendaye Beach is one of the most famous beaches in the Basque Country. Indeed, this beach has the particularity of being very long and made of fine sand, which is very pleasant to lay your towel and sunbathe all afternoon. Moreover, this beach has the particularity of not knowing many waves, which is rare in the Basque Country and very convenient to learn surfing there. Finally this beach offers a magnificent view on a rocky block detachment called "Les Deux Jumeaux": this is the photo that I could take at the top of this page.

Next to the Spain

The city of Hendaye is located in the French Baque-country, on the border between France and Spain, which is very convenient for visiting some Spanish cities such as San Sebastian and eating some delicious tapas. Prices are also generally cheaper than in France, which is why it can be advantageous to bring a few bottles or cigarettes if you smoke.
