Sunrise

On my last day in South Africa, I decided to watch the sunrise from the top of Lion’s Head. The result: an unforgettable show!

Sunset

Many people, families, friends, athletes, climb lion’s head in the evening to contemplate a magnificent sunset while drinking a beer. Good atmosphere and scenery guaranteed!

Wally's cave

The secret cave of Lion's Head is really hard to find, but not difficult to access. While the hiking trail to get to the top can be crowded, the manant path to this magnificent cave is when to it desert. The reason: nobody knows him or how to find him. It took me a long time to find it.
To access it, you will have to cross a fence just before the first ladder then walk along the edge for about 5 minutes, then you will have to descend slightly on the path. At the end of it when the mountain turns on the other side, you should see a cellar at the top of the road, facing Table Mountain.
