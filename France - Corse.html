Piana

Piana is for me one of the most beautiful city of Corsica. Located in the heart of a UNSECO World Heritage Site, its cliffs, its landscapes and this charming little village will not leave you indifferent. Find out more by watching my video.

L'île Rousse

The red island, small town of balagne is ideally located between the Cap Corse to the north, the town of Calvi to the west and the mountains allowing access to the mythical hiking trail of the GR20, considered as the most difficult hike in Europe. Discover beautiful beaches and breathtaking hikes.

Bonifacio

Bonifacio is the southernmost city of Corsica. Diving into the Mediterranean Sea, the houses are built sometimes above the void, would you like to live there?
