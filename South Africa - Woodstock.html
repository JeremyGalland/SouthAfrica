Woodstock

An emerging neighbourhood

The market

Known as The Old Biscuit Mill Market in Woodstock’s popular neighbourhood, this Saturday morning market is one of the trendiest in Cape Town.

The biscuit factory

Built in an old biscuit factory, it is divided into two parts. The first with its shops and stands selling clothes and jewelry made by local artisans in the neighborhood’s workshops. And the second for when hunger calls you!
