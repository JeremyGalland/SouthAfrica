Paris and its surroundings

Paris

Paris, a city known throughout the world, is the capital of France. It would be impossible to list here all that it is possible to do there as long as the list would be. Here is a non-exhaustive list of things to do.

  • The Eiffel Towr
  • The Arc de Triomphe
  • The Sacré Coeur
  • The Tour Montparnasse
  • The Cathédrale Notre Damme de Paris
  • The défense
  • The Jardin du Luxembourg

Rambouillet

Rambouillet is the nearest big city to the village where I live. There are many things to do such as strolling through the famous and very large forest of Rambouillet, or visit the castle of Rambouillet.
Many historical monuments can also be visited near Rambouillet such as the Abbey of Vaux de Cerney.

Chartres

Chartres is a town south west of Paris, after Rambouillet. In the heart of the fields stretching as far as the eye can see, it is in this charming medieval town that I realized my two years of preparatory studies, the opportunity to observe this city by all seasons.
Thus, you can find nearly a dozen churches in this city, from the smallest chapel to the famous and splendid cathedral of Chartres located at the highest point of the city, it is impossible to miss it!
At the back, a beautiful French garden will allow you to enjoy the sun on sunny days.

The city also organizes every year in September a party called "Color Chatres" which is a kind of color run inspired by the Indian tradition of throwing colored powder.
Don’t miss this event!
