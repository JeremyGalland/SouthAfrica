Netherlands

Discover an other culture

Arrival at the youth hostel

To get to the Netherlands, we took the bus between Paris and Amsterdam: 10am by car! Arrived in Amsterdam, we had to discover the local culture in terms of travel modes in order to get to our hostel. We were then able to discover the Amsterdam metro, very different from that of Paris!

Exploring the city

Having arrived in the morning, we were able to enjoy the day by going to rent bicycles for these three days of visit. This allowed us to discover the famous canals of amsterdam, which we then discovered by pedal boat, a really fun and fun way to discover this beautiful city

Time with friends

The evening was an opportunity to spend time at the hostel with my friends by making games such as billiards or card games. A real pleasure to be able to meet his friends after a year of demanding work that had not allowed me to see them.
