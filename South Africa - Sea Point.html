The promenade

The most interesant place in Sea Point is without contest the promenade.
This long walk of 3Km in front of the sea is borrowed by a lot of families, runner or tourists.

This promenade is also the place of interisting monuments or work of art to see.
The fist one is the "Rhinosaur, by André Carl Van der Merwe.
This artwork known as the Rhinosaur was first shown at Afrikaburn in timber and was a result of a group effort.


Rhinosaur by André Carl Van der Merwe.

Overlooking the Atlantic Ocean, an other workart, titled Perceiving Freedom, stares out at Robben Island, home to the prison where Mandela
was incarcerated for nearly three decades.
