Last day

A chill day

On the last day we were able to enjoy a very relaxed afternoon in one of Amsterdam’s many parks. We then took the bicycles back to the city for a last time, such as at the beautiful flower market or along the canals. Finally, we went back with a lot of nostalgia to our bikes then we went back to the hostel before leaving Amsterdam in the early morning.
