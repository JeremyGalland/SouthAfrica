Rothéneuf

Rothéneuf is a small town in Brittany located by the sea, it is on this beach that I went to the sea the first time and I had not returned there in many years. If you want to enjoy a beautiful secluded and quiet beach, this is where you need to go. Don’t forget to bring with you delicious pastries called "Phares Bretons", there is a bakery right next door!

Saint Lunaire

Saint Lunaire is famous for being a very pleasant seaside town in Brittany. Its rocky coasts stretch for many kilometers and offer spectacular panoramas, but also mysterious and full of legends, you are not at the shelter of meeting a rock in the shape of a small monster.
