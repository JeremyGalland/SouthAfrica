Marseille

The phocean city

Church

The church Notre Damme de la Garde is the most famous and beautiful church of Marseille.
At the top of a hill overlooking the old harbour, this church dedicated to sailors offers a splendid view of the whole city and its surroundings. Whatever your religion, this is undoubtedly what you must visit first in Marseille!

Old harbour

The second thing to visit is undoubtedly the old port of Marseille. Go for a worm on the terrace at one of the many bars of the port, this will allow you to better withstand the heat

The coves

Marseille is also known for its numerous creeks and creeks accompanied by small fishing villages. Take the opportunity to swim in turquoise and very hot water.
