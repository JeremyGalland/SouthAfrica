A'DAM tour & red-light district

A'DAM tour

On this second day, we went to the A'DAM tour, which is a very touristic tour of the port of Amsterdam. For example, you can shop on the ground floor, but also take a very impressive elevator with light and sound effects to get to the top. You can then have a beer or a coffee (or both) to enjoy a splendid view of all of Amsterdam.

A very popular attraction is the swing above the void, thrills guaranteed!

Red-light district

At nightfall, we go to the famous red district of Amsterdam to enjoy the very festive atmosphere is very good child who reigns there.
